Welcome to Pesonal Financial Consultant

Our aim is to procure the best possible energy solutions for our clients whilst maintaining strong relationships with our suppliers. Enabling us to provide a quality service, based on value and trust.

You choose the contract that’s right for you and Jim Morrison Utilities Consultant will take care of every requirement from negotiation to overall management of your contract from beginning to end, saving you time, hassle and money.

We will take the hassle out of switching by listening to your needs and negotiating the best value contract on your behalf, saving you up to 25% of your energy costs in the first year alone.

It’s sometimes easy to forget when your energy contract is up for renewal, but getting rolled over onto a standard tariff can be costly.

To ensure you don’t get rolled over into a new, more expensive energy contract, just complete the form and we will be in touch.

We can contact you before your termination date to make sure you don’t get rolled over and secure your business the best possible deal, allowing a seamless transfer at the end of the contract from the old to new supplier, or sometimes even the same supplier but a better deal.
